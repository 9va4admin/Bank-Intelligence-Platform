The Problem ASTRA 03 / 11
IET — The New Mandate

Miss the deadline. Pay regardless.

RBI's Jan 2026 Item Expiry Time rule creates a 3-hour hard window per inward cheque. A missed IET is a deemed approval — the presenting bank must pay even if the cheque is fraudulent.

⏰

The T+3h Hard Stop

Every inward cheque must receive a file-or-return decision within 3 hours of presentation. No grace period, no extension. The clock starts at NGCH receipt.

🔗

Legacy Queues Create Bottlenecks

Current CTS platforms process batches sequentially. A slow fraud check on cheque #1 delays the entire batch of 500. A single system hiccup cascades into IET risk for all.

👤

Human Review Can't Scale

High-value and suspicious cheques require human sign-off. Manual queues have no IET awareness — reviewers don't see a countdown. Escalations arrive too late to act.
