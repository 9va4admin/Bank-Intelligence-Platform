⚠ RBI IT Framework — Vision AI Kill Switch
RBI mandates an operational kill switch on every AI system. ASTRA implements two modes: KP (Kill Partial) — Vision AI runs but STP is suppressed; all outcomes → HUMAN_REVIEW. KC (Kill Complete) — Qwen2-VL bypassed entirely at detect_alteration entry; no GPU cycles consumed. Three scopes: GLOBAL (all instruments), SB_OWN (sponsor bank instruments only), SMB (one specific sub-member). Most restrictive wins: NONE < KP < KC.

Config keys (Layer 3 — maker-checker, hot-reload <30s, Immudb-audited): cts.vision_ai.kill_mode.global   cts.vision_ai.kill_mode.sb_own   cts.vision_ai.kill_mode.smb.{smb_id}

Channels in ASTRA (no SMS — WhatsApp is the urgent channel)
Email via Postal SMTP
Self-hosted MTA. React Email templates. For ops_manager, bank_it_admin, compliance_officer, ml_engineer, fraud_analyst. All P0/P1 events. Delivery tracked in notifications table.
WhatsApp via Meta WA Business API
Official pre-approved templates only. Used for P0 and P1 events where immediate human attention is needed. Recipients: ops_reviewer (IET risk), ops_manager, bank_it_admin (P0 only). Never for bulk or informational events.
Bell In-App (Real-Time)
WebSocket push to active sessions. On disconnect: persisted to notifications table, shown as badge on next login. Primary channel for ops_reviewer during clearing hours (10AM–4PM). Never used for P0 as sole channel.
